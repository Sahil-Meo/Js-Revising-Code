Bluetooth wireless earbuds are Ultra-light and compact, these cordless Bluetooth earbuds are perfect for workouts, runs, sports and gaming or just listening to music and movies. The in-ear earbuds are ergonomically designed to fit snugly into each ear.

 

Earbuds are compatible with Apple iOS, Samsung Galaxy Android, and any Bluetooth enabled device.

 

One step pairing with just simply take the earbuds out of the charging case and the earbuds will automatically enter discovery mode.

 

Don't worry about having a low battery on your earbuds. These earbuds come with a charging case, which allows you to charge the earphones multiple times.

 

Touch sensors with simple tap controls that feature a sleek buttonless user interface, easily manage your playlist, and calls by simply tapping your earbuds.

 

Premium Stereo sound that is crystal clear music and hands-free microphone which allows phone calls with exceptional accuracy and clarity.

 

Earbuds and charging case have waterproof sealed protection.
