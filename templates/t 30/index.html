Product Details

eBay Responsive store with matching responsive listing template 2018!!

 

Your are buying this package which includes this design for your ebay store and listing template

This theme has been professionally developed by our design team and comes in different colours as standard for you to choose from (or we can change the colours to suit your branding/choice). This is a great way to display your products in a professional way without the costs of a completely customised design. Ideal for anyone who currently uses a cheap or free template surround. This will give your business the professional look that is so important nowadays on eBay to increase your sales.

Whats included

  • Responsive Listing template to use for your products in this theme/design.
  • Dynamic matching eBay store design. Updates automatically as you add products and update categories.
  • Choice of colours OR we can change the colours to match your branding or your preference.
  • Change header images to personalise and reflect your business.
  • Social buttons on top. Can be linked to your social pages or removed if not needed.
  • Support, Delivery and Payment banners wording/logos changed as required or removed if not needed.
  • Company name and slogan changed to your business name or your logo incorporated if you have one.
  • As many 'boxes' as you need in the main area for description, terms, images, delivery info etc. You can easily change the name of each box as needed. Content in boxes expands as you type.
  • Extra pages if required.
  • No hidden cost!!!
 

Features

  • Dynamic Categories, sub categories (In stores only).
  • Choice of colours or personalise to your own colours.
  • Compatible with all major browsers, mobile devices and tablets.
  • Easy to use - no HTML or technical knowledge needed.
  • No ongoing costs.
  • Free hosting for your template.
  • Compatible with all the popular ebay listing tools e.g Auctiva, Blackthorne, Channel Grabber, Garage Sale, InkFrog, Turbo Lister, eBay Selling Manager, Channel Advisor, StoreFeeder etc.. (please feel free to email us if you want to check if a particular listing tool is compatible).
  • Works on eBay sites e.g eBay UK, USA, Australia, Germany etc..

How It Works

  • Purchase and Pay
  • We will email you with some questions about your preferences, banner wording, colours etc..
  • Once you have returned these details to us we will send you your template and we will install your new store design within 48 hours (usually within 1 working day)
  • Use our instructions to create your listings and request support if needed.
