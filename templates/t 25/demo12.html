Delivery Info

All orders are wrapped using discreet packaging for peace of mind.

For fraud prevention reasons we can only send your item to the address on your eBay order or Paypal – if you need to change the address once ordered please let us know so we can cancel the sale allowing you to reorder with the correct information.

Free postage may exclude UK Highlands and NI, depending on postcode. Any additional charges will be shown at checkout.

If ordering a large quantity of heavy items (e.g soils, tents or light kits) your order may be sent using a pallet service, which can take an additional couple of days to reach you. Please message us prior to ordering if you do not wish to receive your items on a pallet.

Payment

Payment is expected within 48 hours of auction's end, by PayPal only please.

Returns

Returns are gladly accepted, item must be in same condition as received. Buyer is responsible for all return shipping charges. Please contact us prior to returning an item. Unfortunately opened items could not be returned.

Feedback

Feedback will be given once the item has shipped and we kindly ask that you leave feedback once the item has been received.
