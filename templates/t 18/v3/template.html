Product Details

Send that someone special a romantic Message in a Bottle gift!

Your Gift Includes:

  • one glass corked bottle (approx. 4.7' / 12 cm high (+cork) X 1.3' / 3.5 cm)
  • one red fabric Heart padded
  • mix of white & red sand pebbles
  • one paper rose
  • one blank paper scroll or *handwritten paper scroll tied with gold braid and decorated with a natural seashell
  • handwritten paper scroll - please send me your message in up to 200 characters
  • presented in a jute fabric drawstring pouch gift bag tied with a vintage heart

If you want your own wording handwritten, I can write approximately 200 words or ask for a blank scroll.
You can send your scroll details via the 'Note to seller' box on the checkout page (Please ensure you click the Save Message button, before sending) or you can send us a message via eBay messaging.
